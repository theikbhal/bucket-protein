How to Feed Ducks & Chickens

  • Grow duckweed/azolla in shallow water buckets or ponds.
  • Harvest regularly, rinse clean before feeding.
  • Offer in troughs or shallow dishes mixed with regular feed.
  • Do not feed if it grew in polluted water.

Tips for Growing Feed Plants

  • Use nutrient-rich water (fish water or diluted fertilizer).
  • Keep containers in bright indirect light for fastest growth.
  • Harvest before the mat gets too thick to avoid decay.
  • Azolla also fixes nitrogen, benefiting growth without extra fertilizer. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Safety & Nutrition Notes

  • Do **not** use duckweed/azolla from polluted ponds.
  • Best used as **supplemental** feed with balanced nutrients.
  • Introduce slowly to avoid digestive upset in chickens.
